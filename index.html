AI For Everyone

The AI for Everyone course (Andrew Ng) introduces AI in simple terms, focusing on what AI can and cannot do. It explains AI’s role in business, how projects are built using data, and the importance of collaboration between technical teams and domain experts. The course highlights real-world applications, challenges like bias and privacy, and the societal impact of automation. It empowers non-technical professionals to understand, manage, and apply AI responsibly in their organizations and daily lives.

Introduction to Artificial Intelligence (AI)

The Introduction to Artificial Intelligence (AI) course (IBM, Coursera) covers AI fundamentals, including machine learning, deep learning, neural networks, and generative AI. It explores applications in natural language processing, computer vision, robotics, and autonomous systems. Learners gain insight into AI’s business uses, limitations, and ethical considerations such as bias and fairness. Through examples and hands-on labs, the course equips learners with foundational knowledge to understand, evaluate, and apply AI responsibly in real-world contexts.

AI Essentials

This beginner-level course covers the fundamentals of AI for non-technical roles. It explains what AI is, why it’s relevant now, and how typical AI projects work. Learners also practice tailoring conversations to customers, understanding different AI personas, and starting discovery discussions. No prior experience is needed.

Python for Data Science, AI & Development

This course introduces Python programming for data science, AI, and development. Learners gain hands-on experience with Python basics, data structures, and libraries like Pandas and NumPy. It covers using APIs, web scraping, and working with Jupyter notebooks. Through practical labs and projects, students build skills to manipulate data, analyze datasets, and prepare for advanced topics in machine learning and AI. No prior programming experience is required, making it ideal for beginners pursuing data and AI careers.

Building AI Powered Chatbots Without Programming

This course teaches learners to design and build AI-powered chatbots without coding, using IBM Watson Assistant. It covers creating conversational flows, training the bot to understand user intents, and integrating chatbots into websites or messaging platforms. Learners also explore adding features like context handling and multi-turn conversations. Through practical labs, they gain hands-on experience deploying functional chatbots, preparing them to apply conversational AI in real-world business scenarios without requiring programming skills.
